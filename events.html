Trig Point Massage at sporting events

Here is a collection of the sporting events where I have provided sports massage therapy.

Wasdale X Triathlon

I provided pre-event massages from 2-8pm at the incredible Wasdale X Triathlon. As the rain turned to glorious sunshine during the afternoon, I massaged athletes in preparation for the following day's ultra-event - an Iron Man triathlon in the Lake District: 2.4 mile swim in Wasdale, 112 mile bike ride (Fred Whitton route!), 26 mile fell run including ascent of Scafell Pike.

  • Trig Point Massage is the dark blue marquee here outside the Wasdale Head Inn

  • The start of the swim at 4:30am - the water was 12C, looked dramatic

  • A view back to Wasdale Head Inn from the start of the run up Scafell Pike

Three Rings of Shap

This year's Three Rings of Shap attracted the ultra-runners of runfurther.com, who ran the 62 mile course! It was a long day as I provided pre-event massages Saturday morning, followed by post-event massages throughout the night from 7pm Sat to 10am Sunday. It was hugely inspiring and a great chance to massage elite athletes in a challenging event.
