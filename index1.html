Welcome to Trig Point Massage

Trig Point Massage provides sports massage therapy on the eastern edge of the Lake District.

As a qualified sports massage therapist I provide treatment in the Shap area and at events in and around the Lake District. I frequently treat runners, cyclists, swimmers and long distance walkers to help keep them in top condition.

I have a permanent facility in Shap, which is perfect for Coast 2 Coast walkers needing relaxation and attention to sore muscles. I also have a mobile facility to provide therapy at sports events.

Depending on your requirements, choose massages from:

  • Pre-event: aimed at warming you up and preparing muscles for action
  • Post-event: a relaxing massage to rehabilitate tired muscles
  • Maintenance: in-depth assessment and treatment to keep you in peak condition during training and competition

Don't hesitate to contact me to book a massage or find out more.
