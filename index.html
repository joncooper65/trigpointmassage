Don't hesitate to contact me to book a massage or find out more.

Sports massage

Maintenance massage

A thorough study of your posture, muscles and sporting profile to identify areas that may cause you problems if not released, stretched and strengthened. Typically one hour in duration. Expect some discussion of your training, lifestyle and any soft tissue problems. This is followed by palpation of your muscles to determine a massage routine specific to your needs. Routine Maintenance massages should enable you to train longer and harder and stay injury free. This is a chance for you to learn about your muscles and to be more involved in keeping them in peak condition.

Pre-event

A short, lively and energising massage aimed at helping your warm-up and preparing your muscles for action. It will be done on the day of the event, taking about 10 mins. Alternatively, you can have a longer massage (30 mins) up to two days before the event.

Post-event

This is a relaxing massage of 10-30 mins aimed at flushing toxins and lactic acid from muscles reducing delayed onset muscle sorness (DOMS) and improve recovery time.
